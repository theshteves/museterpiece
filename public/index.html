MUSEterpiece: An iOS Application

The more you think the more artistic static you create.

THE TEAM:

Josh Miles, Steven Kneiser, and Nina Dermody

THE TECH:

MUSE: The Brain Sensing Headband along with X Code and github
THE APPLICATION:
Traditionally used to help during meditation, the MUSE headband takes data from your brain and organizes it in three ways. It separates your brain activity into calm, neutral, or active. Using this data you will be able to see a visual representation of what your brain is doing. Use the image to see how well you can stay calm. Working on anger management or stress maintenance? Visuals can help you really feel that improvement. Tried MUSE before but the sound did the opposite of what you wanted and just distracted you? Try out this app! Try it out and let us know what other uses you have come up with for our app.
