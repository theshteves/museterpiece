MUSEterpiece: An iOS Application

Reading your mind and creating a 3D work of art.

THE TEAM:

Josh Miles, Steven Kneiser, and Nina Dermody

THE TECH:

MUSE: The Brain Sensing Headband along with X Code and github
THE APPLICATION:
Traditionally used to help during meditation, the MUSE headband takes data from your brain and organizes it in three ways. It separates your brain activity into calm, neutral, or active. Using this data you will be able to see a 3D representation of what your brain is doing. Use the 3D image to see how well you can stay calm. Working on anger management or stress maintenance? Visuals can help you really feel that improvement. Try it out and let us know what other uses you have come up with for our app.
